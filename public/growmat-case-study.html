GROWMAT

Enterprise Resource Management Platform
Product Management Case Study by Samuel Zhang @ Pfizer UK

Executive Summary

Led end-to-end product development of GROWMAT, a mission-critical enterprise resource management and forecasting platform that transformed workload management for Pfizer's Global Analytics team.

Through comprehensive user research, cross-functional team leadership, and iterative development, delivered a scalable solution serving 40+ employees across multiple teams, replacing a legacy Excel-based system with modern web-based architecture.

Role: Lead Product Manager & Technical Lead

Duration: January 2024 - Current

Team Size: Cross-functional Agile team

Key Achievements

1200% Efficiency Improvement
120+ Person-Hours Saved Monthly
99.9% System Uptime
40+ Active Users

Problem Definition & User Research

Business Challenges Identified

  • Uncertainties in workload distribution after business restructuring
  • Legacy Excel-based solution didn't scale, prone to errors, computationally slow
  • Lack of granularity in reporting - managers couldn't identify specific bottlenecks
  • Manual processes taking significant analyst time
  • Multiple concurrent users causing system conflicts and downtime

Strategic Solution Approach

  • Comprehensive stakeholder interviews and user research
  • Modern web-based architecture with real-time capabilities
  • Automated data aggregation from multiple enterprise systems
  • Advanced visualization through Spotfire integration
  • Robust data integrity and user permission systems
